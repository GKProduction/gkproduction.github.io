GKProduction Community Rules

Last update: March 15, 2026

About rules

For general order and culture in the GKProduction Community, rules have been established that you should familiarize yourself with before starting your activity here. Please read these rules and follow them!

These rules apply to every GKProduction Community, where publications and actions by community are allowed, including profiles of developers and creators.

Rules

🗑️ 1. Do not spam or flood, do not leave messages and posts that have no sense, keep the community clean. Do not clutter chats, channels, groups, etc. with a bunch of emojis, copypasta, mentions, stickers, links, or repeated messages.

1.1. Do not insert many images and stickers in a row in chats. This takes up a lot of space and hinders viewing messages.

1.2. Do not create many posts and threads in a short time, including meaningless ones. Such posts will be deleted.

1.3. Do not mention (ping) people excessively. Respect others' busyness, unavailability, or simply personal space.

🧐 2. Do not violate generally accepted ethical and moral norms. Observe netiquette.

2.1. Do not use profanity in any form (even if part of the word or the word itself is completely hidden). Swearing, abusive language, and foul language are not welcome in our community.

2.2. Publishing media with profanity is allowed only in specially designated places, specified in the community itself. Keep in mind, not every GKProduction Community has such places.

🤝 3. Be polite, respect your interlocutors and community members.

3.1. Do not insult people or mock anyone. Do not allow any humiliation, discrimination, sexism, racism, Nazism, or hatred. Be tolerant.

3.2. Behave appropriately and adequately. Do not behave toxically or irritably towards server members. Fooling around is also not welcome.

3.3. Do not conduct, support, or provoke conflicts in the community.

3.4. Do not disclose personal information if you have no right to do so. Such information may also include messages from your or others' private conversations, messages from private servers, etc.

3.5. Do not make noise in voice channels and calls, try not to interrupt anyone. Excessive playback of various sounds in voice channels is prohibited.

🚫 4. Avoid incendiary, provocative, and prohibited topics.

4.1. Do not touch upon sensitive topics, politics, religion, race, sexuality, drugs, do not publish NSFW content, as well as material that may cause shock or disgust. Images of a vulgar nature are also prohibited.

4.2. Vile, unpleasant, as well as black humor and other trash are unacceptable. Publishing media with such contents is allowed only in specially designated places, specified in the community itself. Keep in mind, not every GKProduction Community has such places.

4.3. It is prohibited to distribute and publish links to suspicious websites or materials that may contain viruses, unwanted content, as well as to fraudulent resources.

4.4. It is prohibited to distribute and publish links to any pirated content, including resources where games and other materials are published unofficially (for example, outdated mobile versions of GKProduction games).

4.5. It is prohibited to promote illegal modification of applications, as well as their hacking and decompilation (this also applies to web resources).

4.6. Spoilers for video games, movies, and books that have appeared no earlier than two weeks ago are prohibited without hiding and without warning.

📣 5. Do not advertise anything or engage in self-promotion on the server without obtaining permission.

ℹ️ Mentioning a brand or product is not advertising, however, intentional native advertising still falls under this rule.

🌪️ 6. Do not obstruct the work of moderators, do not avoid punishments.

6.1. Do not join the community with multiple accounts without extreme necessity.

6.2. Do not provoke or encourage other people to violate the rules or commit any other bad deeds.

📖 7. Comply with Terms of Service and rules of the social network or web resource.

📜 8. Follow special rules, if such exists. In some places rules are wriitten differently or have extra rules. These rules overlap, but don't replace general rules of GKProduction Community.
